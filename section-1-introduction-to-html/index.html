The Best Movies According to Roberto

My top 3 movies of all time.

Blade Runner 2049

Thoughtful. Introspective. Dystopic. This is a neo-noir exploration of solitude and the pursuit of meaning in one's life. It also contains philosophical questions about how humanity views and interact with AIs.

Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

Bittersweet. Emotional. Touching. This is a creative and powerful take on romance and its tropes. Surrealism acts as the cherry on the cake.

The Seventh Seal

Bold. Poetic. Grim. This film can be a deeply personal experience for some. It proves that the best religious movies might yet be the godless ones.
